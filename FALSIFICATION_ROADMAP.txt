Phase AB — Per-Dispute Effort Rewards (Phase Y safeguard)       CONFIRMED ✅
  Hypothesis: reward-multiplier ≥ tier-complexity keeps defection < 20%
  Result: HOLDS. Minimum viable multiplier = 2× base fee.
  At 1× (no multiplier): high-complexity tier breaches 20% threshold.
  At 2× or higher: all tiers safe.
  Constraint: reward-multiplier must be at least equal to complexity tier (2× for medium).
  Design recommendation: Implement tiered fee schedule — 1× low, 2× medium, 4× high.

Phase AC — Trust Floor + Emergency Onboarding (Phase Z safeguard)  FAILED ❌
  Hypothesis: trust floor keeps legitimacy ≥ 0.50 across 50 epochs
  Result: DOES NOT HOLD under baseline params (100 disputes/epoch).
  Threshold search (fee × emergency × trust): 0/36 configurations viable.
  Structural constraint found:
    At 100 disputes/epoch with resolver-capacity=15, backlog pressure = 0.667.
    Exit-probability bottoms out at ~84% even with emergency=8 and trust=0.8.
    The trust floor onboards resolvers faster than exits at emergency=8, but
    legitimacy never clears 0.159 — well below the 0.50 collapse threshold.
  Root cause CONFIRMED: constraint is structural, NOT a model artefact.
    Rolling 5-epoch average applied to backlog pressure (April 2026 revision);
    0/36 result unchanged. Pressure at 100 disputes/epoch is persistently high
    (0.667) regardless of smoothing — not a transient spike.
  Design implication: Trust floor requires resolver capacity expansion:
    resolver-capacity × pool-size ≥ 10× disputes-per-epoch
    (option (b) rolling-average already applied and confirmed insufficient)
  Status: Structural constraint confirmed real. Safeguard design requires
    capacity expansion before trust floor can be effective.

Phase AD — Governance Bandwidth Floor (Phase AA safeguard)      CONFIRMED ✅
  Hypothesis: mandatory floor reviews keep attacker win rate < 20%
  Result: HOLDS at minimum viable configuration.
  Threshold search (floor-reviews × total-capacity):
    Minimum viable: floor=2, capacity=3 → 18.4% win rate.
    Analytical derivation confirmed: floor > n/2 required (floor ≥ 3 of 5).
    Empirical minimum (18.4%) is at the edge — floor=3, capacity=3 is safer (16%).
  Recommended config: floor=3 reviews per epoch, total-capacity=3-4.
  Constraint: floor < n/2 is insufficient regardless of total capacity.
  Design recommendation: Mandate minimum 60% coverage of low-value disputes per epoch.
