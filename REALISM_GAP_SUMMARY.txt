THE 6 BIGGEST GAPS (By Severity)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. BRIBERY MARKETS (HIGHEST RISK)
   Gap: Your sim assumes bribery is always detected or too expensive
   Reality: Contingent bribes (p+ε) change the game:
     - Attacker offers: "Vote X, or we collateralize in escrow"
     - Juror sees: "Profit if attack succeeds, no loss if fails"
     - Cost: May be 30–50% of escrow (not infinity)
   Impact: -40% confidence if not addressed

2. EVIDENCE GAMES (HIGHEST RISK)
   Gap: Your sim assumes evidence quality is binary
   Reality: Attackers can forge evidence, witnesses can withhold
     - Deepfakes, selective screenshots, translation tricks
     - Verification time >> forge time
     - Under time pressure, jurors use heuristics (narrative wins)
   Impact: -30% confidence if honest evidence > fake costs

3. PARTICIPATION DECAY (MEDIUM RISK)
   Gap: Your sim has exogenous juror supply
   Reality: Jurors fatigue, quit, or get adverse-selected
     - Long queue → only risk-seeking jurors remain
     - Slowness → honest users leave → security drops
     - Reflexive spiral: slowness → exit → lower stake → attack
   Impact: -20% confidence if queue effects are severe

4. LEGITIMACY COLLAPSE (MEDIUM RISK)
   Gap: Your sim assumes trust is constant
   Reality: Trust is path-dependent and fragile
     - Perceived bias → exodus threat
     - Variance in outcomes → users leave
     - Once exodus starts, hard to reverse
   Impact: -20% confidence if legitimacy is fragile

5. GOVERNANCE CAPTURE (MEDIUM RISK)
   Gap: Your sim models governance as black box with fixed delay
   Reality: Governance can be captured via rule drift or timing
     - Repeated small rule changes → predictable patterns
     - Attacker times attack for low-salience period
     - Governance can't move fast enough
   Impact: -15% confidence if governance is predictable

6. LEARNING ATTACKERS (MINOR-MEDIUM RISK)
   Gap: Your sim has static attacker (doesn't learn)
   Reality: Real attackers optimize over epochs
     - Epoch 1: Try bribe → fails (20% success)
     - Epoch 2: Try evidence → succeeds (45% success)
     - Epoch 3+: Allocate budget to evidence (converge to optimum)
   Impact: -10% confidence if attacker finds <20% cost strategy
