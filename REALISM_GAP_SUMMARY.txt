   Gap was: Attackers forge evidence faster than resolvers verify

   Result: Kleros detection covers 100% of forgery attempts with adequate time

   Result: System viable at 25–30+ resolvers; critical mass at 15 (brittle)
   Residual risk: Below 20 resolvers approaches brittle zone — monitor at launch
